Rainwell

Category: Design / Art

Tools Used: Rhino, Illustrator

Project date: December 18, 2024

Project Description

This was the final project for 4.021 (How to Design). Our team created a 70-foot inflatable installation inside MIT's Building 3 stairwell to explore stress relief through sound, scent, and soft interactive design.

We integrated blue lighting, ambient rain audio, and essential oil scents into the installation. Our intent was to encourage students to look up, pause, and interact with the space—especially during finals. The piece transformed a busy vertical corridor into a sensory moment of calm and playfulness.
